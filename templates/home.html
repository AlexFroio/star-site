Space-Time Adventures at RIT (STAR) is RIT's own science fiction club! STAR hopes to bring student sci-fi enthusiasts together to share and enjoy TV and film series such as Doctor Who, Star Trek, Battlestar Galactica, Star Wars, Firefly, and many more through club meetings, screenings, and other events.

STAR runs weekly missions every Monday night from 7:00-9:00 PM in Campus Center room 2510/2560, as well as various other special missions throughout the school year.
